How it works

Based on an heuristic evaluation we have created a complete guide of instructions and questions for you to make to any chatbot in order to evaluate its design. All these questions will help you find problems in the human-chatbot interaction, and will give you an overview about where to focus divided into 7 categories.

Learn more

The 7 categories on chatbot design

Personality
Does the chatbot have a clear voice and tone that fits with the users and with the ongoing conversation?

Onboarding
Are users understanding was the chatbot about and how to interact with him from the very beginning?

Understanding
Requests, smalltalk, idioms, emojis... What the hell is the chatbot capable to understand?

Answering
What elements does the chatbot send and how well it is doing it? Are they relevant to the moment they are used?

Navigation
How easy is to go through the chatbot conversation? Do you fell lost sometimes while spaking with the chatbot?

Error manage
How good is the chatbot dealing with all the errors that are going to happen? Is able to recover from them?

Intelligence
Does the chatbot have any intelligence? Is able to remember things? Uses and manages context as a person?
